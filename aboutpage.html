About the Disaster Preparedness Portal

The Disaster Preparedness Portal is an online platform dedicated to helping individuals, communities, and institutions anticipate, prepare for, and respond to natural and man-made disasters. It serves as a centralized hub that brings together vital information, tools, and best practices for effective disaster management.

This website was developed by a team of IT students from St. Rita’s College of Balingasag as part of their community-oriented initiative. It is specifically designed for Barangay 3, Calacalá, Balingasag, Misamis Oriental, aiming to enhance local preparedness, awareness, and coordination during emergencies. Through this platform, residents can access real-time updates, safety guidelines, and communication resources to ensure a safer and more resilient community.

Our mission is to ensure every resident is informed and ready to respond effectively during emergencies. By combining technology and local coordination, we aim to reduce risks, prevent misinformation, and promote safety across the community.

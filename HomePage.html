About the Disaster Risk Preparedness Portal

The Disaster Risk Preparedness Portal is an online platform dedicated to helping individuals, communities, and institutions anticipate, prepare for, and respond to natural and man-made disasters. The portal serves as a centralized resource hub and coordination tool that brings together information, tools, and best practices for disaster risk management.
